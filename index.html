Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking.

It depends on the individual. In Queensland, learner drivers must complete a minimum of 100 logged hours before sitting their practical test. Most students benefit from a mix of private practice with family and professional lessons with an instructor. We'll give you an honest assessment after your first lesson on what we think you'll need.
We cover Logan Central and surrounding suburbs including Marsden, Loganlea, Meadowbrook, Kingston, Waterford, Crestmead, Beenleigh, Browns Plains, Springwood, Sunnybank, Runcorn, Moorooka, Salisbury, Calamvale and more. Not sure if we cover your area? Just get in touch and we will let you know.
All lessons are in an automatic vehicle. This means you will be eligible for an automatic licence on passing your test. If you specifically need manual, get in touch and we can discuss options.
Bring your current Queensland learner licence. You must have it on you to legally drive on public roads. Wear comfortable, flat shoes. Your logbook is also useful so we can sign off your hours after each session.
Payment is required before your lesson to secure your booking. Your instructor will be in touch after you enquire to confirm your time and arrange payment. We will confirm all pricing before your first lesson so there are no surprises.
We ask for at least 24 hours notice if you need to cancel or reschedule. Cancellations with less than 24 hours notice may incur a fee. We understand things come up so just let us know as soon as possible and we will do our best to work with you.
Yes. We offer two test day packages that include car hire for your practical test. The Car Test Pack Standard ($210) includes a 45 minute lesson before your test, car hire, and pickup and drop off on weekdays. The Car Test Pack Advanced ($295) includes a 105 minute lesson before your test, car hire, and pickup and drop off on weekdays. Get in touch to book either package.

Learning to Drive in Logan Central

Logan Central is one of the busiest hubs in the Logan City area, making it an ideal place to build real-world driving skills. From the shopping precinct along Kingston Road to the surrounding residential streets, learners get exposure to a wide range of road conditions in a compact area.

Local Roads and Test Routes

Lessons in Logan Central cover everything from quieter residential streets for beginners to busier arterials like Wembley Road and Logan Road for more advanced learners. Our instructor is familiar with the local test routes and focuses on the specific manoeuvres and intersections that examiners look for.

Flexible Pickup Across Logan Central

We pick you up from home, school, or wherever suits you in Logan Central and the surrounding area. Lessons are available seven days a week to fit around school, work, and other commitments. Whether you just got your Ls or you're preparing for your practical test, we have a lesson option to suit where you are in the journey.
